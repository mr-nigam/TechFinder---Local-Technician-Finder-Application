Emergency Services

Emergencies include burst pipes, electrical hazards, complete system failures, security breaches, or any situation that poses immediate danger or significant property damage.

Our emergency response team aims to have a technician dispatched within 15 minutes of your call. Actual arrival time depends on your location and technician availability.

Pricing & Payment

Pricing is based on the service type, duration, urgency level, and travel distance. You'll see a detailed breakdown before confirming your booking.

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash payments. Payment is processed securely through our platform.
